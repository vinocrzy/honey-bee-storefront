The Cold Process

We wait six weeks for every bar. This patient curing process ensures a harder, longer-lasting bar with a rich, creamy lather.

Usage Ritual

Elevate your daily cleansing into a moment of intentionality.

01.

Wet the bar and create a lather in your hands or on a natural sea sponge. Breathe in the botanical oils.

02.

Massage gently onto damp skin in circular motions. The clay and honey work to lift impurities while nourishing.

03.

Rinse with cool water. Pat dry and follow with our 'Morning Sun' Botanical Oil to lock in moisture.

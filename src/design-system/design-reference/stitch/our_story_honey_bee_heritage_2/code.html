From Clinical to Craft

The Founder's Journey

For fifteen years, Sarah worked the night shift as a critical care nurse. In the sterile, high-stakes environment of the ICU, she found solace in the science of healing—but her own skin, ravaged by clinical detergents and stress, cried out for a gentler kind of care.

"Healing starts with what we touch."

Our Process

We don't rush nature. Our cold-process method preserves the integrity of our botanical ingredients, requiring six weeks of patient curing to reach peak quality.

Slow-made with intention

The Infusion

Botanical oils are steeped with organic herbs for 48 hours before the soap-making begins.

Cold Saponification

By avoiding external heat, we ensure the delicate vitamins in our wild honey remain active.

The Six-Week Cure

Each bar matures in our temperature-controlled atelier to ensure a hard, long-lasting, luxurious lather.

Radical Sourcing

hive

Wild Meadow Honey

Sourced from a singular family-owned apiary in the Blue Ridge Mountains, our honey is raw, unfiltered, and harvested with the health of the hive as the first priority.

Botanical Alchemy

We use only cold-pressed jojoba, sweet almond, and castor oils—never palm oil. Each ingredient is selected for its specific fatty acid profile to mimic the skin's natural barrier.

The Nurse's Promise

Every bar we make is dermatologically tested, small-batch produced, and formulated with medical-grade precision—wrapped in the luxury of the earth."

Sarah Henderson, RN

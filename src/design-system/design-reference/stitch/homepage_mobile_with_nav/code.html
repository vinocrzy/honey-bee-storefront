Our Heritage

From Care to Craft

Founded by Sarah, a former palliative care nurse, Honey Bee was born from a deep understanding of skin sensitivity and the restorative power of gentle care.

Transitioning from clinical nursing to the alchemy of soap making, Sarah brought with her a commitment to medical-grade purity combined with the slow, tactile rhythm of handmade artistry. Every bar is a tribute to the healing power of nature.
